Quick Guides

📈 How to Buy / Sell Stocks

1. Go to the Invest page.
2. Select a stock (e.g. Tesla, Apple).
3. Enter Amount and Click 'Invest'.
4. To Sell, go to 'Profile' and select 'Portfolio'.

💳 Using Paytm, PhonePe & Mobikwik

To Invest:
1. Select 'UPI' method in Invest.
2. Enter Amount (Min ₹100).
3. We will trigger a request to your Paytm, PhonePe, or Mobikwik app.
4. Approve payment in the app to credit funds instantly.

⚡ How to Get Free Recharges

You can use your Referral Bonus balance to pay for mobile recharges.
Go to UPI > Recharge, enter number, and select 'Pay from Bonus'.
